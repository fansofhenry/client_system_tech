And I genuinely care about the student success mission that drives this institution — my thesis work at both SJSU and Foothill was centered on how technology can serve equity and learning outcomes, not just efficiency.

I'm local, I'm ready, and I'd be proud to represent FHDA's IT team. Thank you for your consideration.
